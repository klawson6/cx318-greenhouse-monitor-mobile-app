Our project works to have a way to monitor the conditions within a greenhouse- conditions such as motion, temperature, light and humidity.
We also set it up so that the user will have the ability to control some of those conditions, and be notified when motion is detected.
 There are also various links in the navigation bar which will take you to the following sections:

Profile Page

This takes you to the page where you can provide an email address and your name in order to be given an update when motion is detected, or allow you to delete the data we have on you


SensorTag Data

This takes you to the page where sensortag data will be displayed, and it will update data every 5 seconds while the sensortag is active.


Weather Data

This will allow you to easily check the local weather conditions with respect to your greenhouse location.
To gather our weather data, we used the Met Office website as a source for our information

Controls

This will take you to a page where you can provide an ESP ID, and then connect to it (if it is active) in order to be able to control some of the greenhouse conditions
